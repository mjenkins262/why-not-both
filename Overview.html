Overview

This dashboard shows rates for Gold Gloves and Silver Sluggers relative to career length. Partial seasons are counted, even if a player only appeared in one game.

The quadrant chart shows each player’s balance of offense and defense. Over a full career, no player ends in the top-right quadrant, but some get there briefly or even with one team.

The leaderboard includes a 0-100 Rating which weighs both awards equally.

The line chart tracks how cumulative rates change each year. The peak for the population is at year 10.

Use the filters to find trends and oddities, or to see how a player's dot travels through the chart over their career.
