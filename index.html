Overview

This dashboard compares Gold Gloves and Silver Sluggers relative to career length. A season counts if a player appeared at all, so partial seasons count.

The quadrant chart shows each player’s balance of offense and defense. Over a full career, nobody stays top-right, but some get there briefly or with one team.

The leaderboard includes a 0-100 Rating which weighs both awards equally.

The line chart tracks how cumulative rates change each year. The peak for the population is at year 10.

Use the filters to find trends and oddities, or to see how a player's dot travels through the chart over their career.
