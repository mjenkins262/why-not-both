10-Year Peak

This dashboard looks at Gold Gloves and Silver Sluggers relative to career length, not just total awards.

The filters help surface both real trends and weird edge cases, like one-season 100% players.

The quadrant chart shows each player’s mix of offense and defense. Over a full career, nobody stays in the top-right zone, though some get there for a while or with one team.

The line chart shows how those rates rise and fall over time. Across the full population, the peak is around year 10.

The leaderboard includes a Rating from 0 to 100 that weights both awards equally.

A season counts if the player appeared at all, so partial seasons count too.
