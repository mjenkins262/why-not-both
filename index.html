Overview

This project looks at offensive and defensive success together using Gold Gloves and Silver Sluggers relative to career length.

Instead of total awards, it focuses on how often players earned them over time. The filters help surface both meaningful trends and edge cases like one-season 100% players.

The quadrant chart groups players by their combination of offense and defense. Over a full career, no player sits in the top-right zone, though some reach it briefly or with a single team.

The line chart shows how those rates change over time. Across the population, performance tends to peak around year 10.

The leaderboard includes a Rating from 0 to 100 that weights both awards equally, creating a single measure of two-way success.
