10-Year Peak

By season 10, many players are near their peak in combined offensive and defensive success. This view shows who had built the strongest two-way profile at that point, rather than over a full career.

This slice is useful because it reduces the advantage of very long careers and brings more attention to players who reached a high level early.

The leaderboard highlights the top ratings at that stage, while the quadrant chart shows who was closest to elite performance in both awards at once.
